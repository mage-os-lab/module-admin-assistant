Credit Memos
Manage credit memos, you can view credit memo details, edit credit memo, print credit memo, send email to customer, send email to admin, send email to customer and admin
Credit Memos are created to refund a customer for a product that was returned. A credit memo is created from an invoice. You can create a credit memo for an entire invoice or for a partial amount. A credit memo can not be created before invoiced.
